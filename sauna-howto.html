Sauna — How To (15–20 min)

  1. Before: hydrate 8–16 oz; remove metal; place towel down.
  2. Set: Infrared 120–140°F; door closed.
  3. Timer: up to 20 minutes; leave early if dizzy or unwell.
  4. Breathe: 4 seconds in / 6 seconds out.
  5. After: cool 5–10 min; re-hydrate + electrolytes; wipe bench.

Contraindications: pregnancy, fever/illness, uncontrolled hypertension, recent cardiac events. Ask your clinician if unsure.
