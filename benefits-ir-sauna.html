Why use IR saunas?

Relax & de-stress

Warmth promotes relaxation and a drop in perceived stress — great on recovery days or after a heavy lift.

Feel looser for mobility

Gentle heat can make mobility work feel easier. Pair your session with our 8-minute Post-Sauna Reset.

Light cardio effect

Mild increase in heart rate and sweating — think easy, passive warm-up. Hydrate and treat it like exercise.

Habit-friendly

Short sessions (15–20 min) 2–4× per week are easy to stack onto training or rest days.

Simple session plan

Before

Hydrate. Remove metal jewelry. Wipe bench. Start low heat if you’re new.

During

Sit or recline comfortably. Breathe easy. Keep it to 15–20 min; leave sooner if dizzy or uncomfortable.

After

Cool down and re-hydrate. Hit the Post-Sauna Reset or our Low-Back Decompress.

Safety & considerations

  • Listen to your body — exit if light-headed, nauseous, or unwell.
  • Extra caution if you’re heat-sensitive, pregnant, have cardiovascular conditions, or take meds that affect sweating. When in doubt, check with a clinician first.
  • Wipe down surfaces after use; place a towel between skin and bench.
  • Minimum age: follow posted policy for minors and supervision.
