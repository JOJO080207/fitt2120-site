Pre-Lift Mobility (5 min)

Quick sequence to prep hips, t-spine and shoulders before you train.

Use nearby gear

  • Technogym Flexability Posterior station (optional 3–5 min)
  • Mini-band & light dumbbells

5-Minute Flow

1) Posterior Chain (1 min)

Flexability Posterior gentle stretch — slow, no bounce. Breathe 4–5 deep breaths.

2) Hip Hinge Pattern (1 min)

KB/Dumbbell RDL patterning: 2×10 light, focus on ribs down + flat back.

3) T-Spine Opener (1 min)

Quadruped thread-the-needle: 8/side with slow reach and breathe out.

4) Shoulder Prep (1 min)

Band pull-apart + face pull: 2×12 each (light band).

5) Squat or Hinge Groove (1 min)

Bodyweight squats or hip airplanes → 10 smooth reps focusing on depth and balance.

Post-Sauna Reset (8 min)

1) Box Breathing (2 min)

4-sec inhale • 4-sec hold • 4-sec exhale • 4-sec hold. Sit or lie supine.

2) Low-Back Decompress (3 min)

Knees-to-chest rocks 10× → dead bug 2×8/side slow.

3) Hips & Hamstrings (3 min)

90/90 hip switches 10× → hamstring strap stretch 30s/side.
