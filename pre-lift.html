Stretch Routines

Pre-Lift Mobility (5 min)

  1. Hamstring hinge — 30s/side ×2
  2. Calf stretch (knee straight) — 30–45s/side
  3. Glute/piriformis — 30–45s/side
  4. T-spine opener on bar — 30–45s
Start 5:00 timer

Post-Sauna Reset (8 min)

  1. Hamstring hinge — 45s/side ×2
  2. Adductors — 45s/side
  3. Glute/piriformis — 45s/side
  4. Low-back decompression — 45s ×2
  5. Breathing — 4-in/6-out × 6 cycles
Start 8:00 timer
