How it works

1) Step on

Remove shoes/socks, stand on the platform, and hold the handles.

2) Scan (60 sec)

Remain still while the analyzer measures impedance across your body.

3) Review & track

FAQ

Is it safe?

Safe for most people. Not recommended for pregnancy or anyone with implanted electronic medical devices. Ask staff if unsure.

What should I wear?

Light clothing; remove shoes and socks. Keep posture relaxed and still during the scan.

How often should I scan?

Baseline on day one, then every 4 weeks to monitor progress.
