FAQ

Is it safe?

Yes for most people. Not recommended for pregnancy or anyone with implanted electronic medical devices. Ask staff if unsure.

What should I wear?

Light clothing; remove shoes and socks. Keep posture relaxed and still during the scan.

How often should I scan?

Baseline on day one, then every 4 weeks for progress tracking.
